Offpeak Deals is a mobile app through which restaurants can incentivize customers to dine-in during the slowest hours of the day, by offering time-dependent, percentage-based dining discounts. By offering deals through OffPeak, your restaurant instantly gains free marketing and exposure to thousands of hungry customers on our platform.
Through the Offpeak Deals Merchant app, built for you, our restaurant partners, you can set and customize a profile for your restaurant, set, customize, and activate percentage-based dining discount offers for specific time frames, and view live data on how customers are using the deals you have set.
On the public-facing app, customers can view all active deals in their geographic area. Customers can then “grab” a deal, and will have 30 minutes to come in to your restaurant and use the deal.
